How to Contact Us:



Call us anytime, Day or Night

Whatever you're going through, you can call us any time, from any phone for FREE.

Phone: 061 354 7653.

Write us an email

You can write us an email and we will respond within 24 hours at:

ULSP@gmail.com

Visit us at our Branch

You're welcome to drop by our branch in the University of Limerick Courtyard anytime between 9:00 and 17:00 Monday to Friday!

Write us a Letter

Sometimes writing down your thoughts and feelings can help you better understand them. You can write to us by freepost to our Branch address.
