Why

There are countless reasons you why you might want to join University Limerick Suicide Prevention , with one of the most evident being that you want to help. You want to help those who need it, those who are struggling. And this trait alone makes you a wonderful person and we would love to have you aboard our team. As you probably know, our main objective is to stop anyone from ending their life prematurely and if you feel that you have what it takes to help and care for those at their most vulnerable, then you will fit in perfectly to our team.

How

There are just a few entry requirements to join ULSP;

  1. Be in moderately good shape and health
  2. Garda vetting
  3. A willingness to do whatever is needed of you in any situation
  4. Previous first aid, defibrillator and/or any other medical training would be a bonus but not necessary
If you meet all of these requirements, just fill out the online form and send it on. Alternatively, you can call us and we can go through the process over the phone at 085 175 4862.

Whats's Involved

We take what we do at ULSP very seriously and expect the utmost dedication and level of sincerity when carrying out our duties. We would like everyone to be able to make it to a minimum of 4 patrol nights out a month if possible (with extenuating circumstances taken into account). Other than that, just know that there will be a lot of walking and cycling around with a lot of downtime. So be prepared for some cold nights.


Everyone is provided with ASIST, First Aid, Defibrillator, Throw Bag and VHF Radio training, as well any other courses that may be deemed relevant throughout the year. Some of these will be mandatory courses (such as First Aid and Defibrillator training), but don’t worry if you can’t make it to one of them as there will be many held throughout the year.


Other than that, the actual patrols mainly consist of monitoring the UL campus (especially the bridge) and ensuring that no one is in obvious distress or seems inclined to take their own life. There will be rotas and everyone will be informed well in advance of there dates. Changes can be made if needed. For any further information, do not hesitate to call at 061 354 7653.

We do hope that you do decide to join us in our worthy cause.
