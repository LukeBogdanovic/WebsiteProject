Q: What do we do?

A: Here in ULSP, we do nightly rounds of walking the UL campus and making sure that the students of UL are safe. We patrol the bridges, such as the living bridge, and watch for any students that are in danger of taking their own lives. While it is a tiring job, it's good to know that you're making a difference here in UL. We have people around campus volunteer for certain days, and we do a patrol on those nights with whoever has volunteered.

Q: Why do we do what we do?

A: We do what we do in order to protect and help the people of UL. Here at ULSP, we aim to provide hope for those going through a tough time. People all around the world lose their loved ones to suicide each day. We're here to help people like that and bring hope into their lives.

Q: What leads people to suicide?

A: There are many factors that can lead people to suicide. For example, there is depression, trauma, isolation, loss and many other reasons. People like this are in a dark place, and need help and guidance in their times of need.

Q: What can I do to help?

A: If you would like to help our cause, we recommend going to the 'Become a Volunteer' tab above to become a volunteer!
